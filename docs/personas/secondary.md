# Persona: The Passive Beginner (Secondary)

## Name
Mark Haze

## Age
28

## Occupation
Junior Software Engineer

## Investment Experience
Beginner (1–2 years)

## Key Characteristics
- Invests small monthly amounts
- Wants guidance on how to build a balanced portfolio
- Not confident enough to make decisions alone
- Attracted to automation and “recommended actions”

## Goals
- Understand whether his portfolio is on track
- Simple alerts that explain the “why”
- Easy insights without financial jargon
- Improve risk–reward balance without micromanaging

## Frustrations
- Confusing charts and metrics in most investment apps
- No help when deciding what to do next
- Fear of making mistakes

## Needs
- Clear explanations
- Simple yes/no automated suggestions
- Beginner-friendly dashboards

## Preferred Devices
Mobile first

## Quote
“Just tell me what needs attention in my portfolio — in simple terms.”
