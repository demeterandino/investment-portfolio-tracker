# Persona: The Automated Investor (Primary)

## Name
Anna Smith

## Age
34

## Occupation
Business Analyst

## Investment Experience
Intermediate (4–6 years)

## Key Characteristics
- Interested in long-term wealth growth
- Tech-savvy and comfortable using finance apps
- Wants automation to reduce manual oversight
- Focuses on ETFs, blue-chip stocks, and long-term strategies
- Prefers set-and-forget investing but still wants visibility

## Goals
- Reduce time spent tracking her portfolio
- Avoid missing important market movements
- Ensure portfolio allocation remains aligned to risk tolerance
- Receive automated insights without overwhelming alerts

## Frustrations
- Manually checking prices and adjusting weights
- Too many irrelevant notifications from other finance apps
- Missing rebalancing opportunities
- Difficulty tracking which assets need attention

## Needs
- Smart alerts she can trust
- Automatic rebalancing suggestions based on rules
- Categorized assets with minimal manual cleanup
- A dashboard that shows only what matters

## Preferred Devices
Mobile (70%), Desktop (30%)

## Quote
“I want my portfolio to stay healthy with minimal effort — tell me only what’s important.”
