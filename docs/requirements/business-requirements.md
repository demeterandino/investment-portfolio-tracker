# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
## Investment Portfolio Tracker — Automation Focus


## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this product is to automate the most time-consuming tasks of portfolio management, including asset categorization, rebalancing, and alert monitoring. The product should reduce manual work, increase portfolio stability, and provide meaningful insights without overwhelming the user.


## 2. Business Objectives
- Reduce portfolio maintenance time by **50%** within 3 months of usage.
- Increase user trust by providing transparent, explainable automation.
- Improve investment decision quality through timely, actionable alerts.
- Support both beginner and intermediate retail investors.
- Increase conversion to premium features (alerts + automation) to **10%** in year 1.


## 3. Key Business Needs
- Accurate and real-time financial data ingestion.
- High-quality, noise-free alerts with relevance filtering.
- Automated rebalancing recommendations aligned with a user’s risk profile.
- Clear explanation for every automated action.
- Secure handling of sensitive investment data.


## 4. Success Metrics
- **Alert Relevance Score > 80%** (measured by user feedback)
- **Monthly Active Users** growth of +15% monthly
- **Automation Opt-in Rate > 40%**
- **User Time Saved / Month > 2 hours**
- **Portfolio Stability Score** improved through diversification metrics


## 5. Target Market
- Retail investors aged 25–45  
- Early adopters of investment apps  
- Users of ETFs, stocks, and long-term portfolios  
- Tech-savvy individuals seeking automation


## 6. Business Constraints
- Financial data must be sourced reliably (Yahoo Finance API or similar)
- Compliance with data protection laws (GDPR)
- Automated actions must be user-approved (no direct trading)
- Explanations must remain simple and non-technical


## 7. Assumptions
- Users will provide accurate initial data or connect APIs.
- Market data availability is reliable and frequent.
- Automation rules can be computed within seconds.


## 8. Out of Scope
- Real trading or broker integration  
- Tax filing automation  
- High-frequency trading features  
- Crypto-only functionality (may be added later)
